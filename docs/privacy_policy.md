# Sirat-i Nur Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-17

Sirat-i Nur is an Islamic lifestyle app for prayer times, Qibla direction,
Quran reading and audio, duas, dhikr, nearby Islamic places, reminders, and
optional premium access. This policy explains what data the app uses and why.

## Data We Use

- Location: Used only when the user enables location-based features such as
  prayer times, Qibla direction, and nearby mosques or halal places. Approximate
  or precise location may be processed by the device and by configured map or
  places providers when the user opens those features.
- Notifications and alarms: Used to schedule prayer reminders and adhan alerts
  at the times selected by the user.
- Purchases: Premium purchases are processed by Google Play or Apple. Sirat-i
  Nur receives purchase status needed to unlock premium features, but does not
  receive full payment card details.
- Chatbot prompts: If the user enables cloud AI mode, the message text is sent
  to the configured AI provider to generate a response. If cloud AI is not
  configured, the app uses local fallback content.
- App content and storage: The app may download or cache Quran audio, duas,
  tafsir, hadith, and other verified Islamic content for offline use.
- Diagnostics: The app may show local configuration and content availability
  diagnostics to help the user understand whether verified content is ready.

## Data We Do Not Sell

Sirat-i Nur does not sell personal data. The app does not use user data for
third-party advertising.

## Third-Party Services

Depending on the enabled features and production configuration, Sirat-i Nur may
use Google Play Billing, Apple in-app purchase systems, Supabase, map tile or
places providers, YouTube embeds for live streams, and Google Gemini for optional
cloud AI responses. These providers process data according to their own terms
and privacy policies.

## Retention

Most app settings and downloaded content are stored locally on the user's
device. The user can remove downloaded content from inside the app or by
uninstalling the app. Purchase history is retained by the relevant app store.

## Children

Sirat-i Nur is intended for a general audience seeking Islamic reference and
practice tools. The app does not knowingly sell or use children's data for
advertising.

## User Choices

Users can deny location permission and still use non-location features. Users can
disable reminders from device settings or from app settings where available.
Users can keep chatbot use in offline mode when they do not want prompts sent to
a cloud AI provider.

## Contact

For privacy requests or support, contact the developer through the Google Play or
App Store listing, or open an issue at:

https://github.com/UmutAmal/Sirat--Nur/issues

## Changes

This policy may be updated when app features, providers, or legal requirements
change. The latest version is published in this repository.
